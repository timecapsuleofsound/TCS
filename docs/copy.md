- **Section Label:** The Origin
- **Heading:** Where It All Started.
- **Body:** Born in Chicago in the early 1980s, House Music was a revolution dressed in rhythm. ## Page 7 — About

### Page Header
- **Headline:** ABOUT TCS
- **Subheadline:** Time Capsule of Sound is a House Music destination built to preserve the past, celebrate the present, and inspire the future.

### Mission Section
- **Section Label:** Our Mission
- **Heading:** Preserving the Sound. Celebrating the Culture.
- **Body:** Time Capsule of Sound — a division of TCS — was founded with one purpose: to make sure the music that shaped generations is never lost. House Music is more than a genre. It's a language, a community, a way of life. We stream it live, archive it, and share it with everyone who wants to listen.

### Story Section
- **Section Label:** Our Story
- **Heading:** From a Vision to a Movement.
- **Body:** It started with a deep love for House Music and a recognition that so much of its history was scattered, forgotten, or locked away in dusty crates and fading cassettes. TCS was built to change that. We built this platform to create a home for the culture — a place where legends are remembered, new artists are heard, and the community finds each other.

### What We Do Section
- **Section Label:** What We Do
- **Heading:** Live. Archive. Connect.
- **Items:**
  - **Stream Live** — We broadcast live DJ sets, events, and exclusive sessions on Twitch and YouTube, bringing the energy of the dance floor directly to you.
  - **Archive the History** — Our library of mixes, sets, and recordings grows every week. From the classics to the cutting edge, it's all here.
  - **Build Community** — TCS is a gathering place for DJs, producers, fans, and anyone who believes in the power of House Music to bring people together.

### Vision Section
- **Section Label:** Where We're Going
- **Heading:** The Future of House Music Lives Here.
- **Body:** We're just getting started. TCS will continue to grow — more streams, more archives, more community features, and more ways to experience the music you love. Stay connected. The best is yet to come.
